    About Me

    I am a Vietnamese-American who embarked on a new journey when I immigrated to the United States in late 2011. The following year, I settled in Garden Grove, CA, a community rich with Vietnamese-American culture, where my family and I found support despite our limited English proficiency.

    In May 2023, I graduated from California State University of Fullerton with Good Standing, earning my B.S. in Computer Engineering. My studies in Embedded Systems and FPGA have equipped me with valuable skills as I explore various opportunities to launch my career in the field. My journey so far is a testament to perseverance, adaptability, and an unending desire to learn and grow.
